How Our Two-Factor Authentication Works

Please note that if your network connection is bad, your authentication code may take more time to reach you.

# Step What to expect/do
1 Enable Two-Factor Authentication In your settings, enable Two Factor Authentication
2 When Logging In
  1. Enter your login credentials
  2. Wait for verification code in your verification device
  3. Type the verification code where it is expected on your screen
  4. You will now be Logged In, you may proceed.
