# Step What to expect Pre-Requisite Step
1 Sign Up You will be asked for your personal information like name and email etc. None
2 Register
  • Provide Personal Details
  • Provide Guardian Details
  • Upload Supporting Documents
  • Add Banking Details for Payment
 You must have Signed Up, and Logged In
3 Waiting Period
  • Wait for an approval of registration SMS or email
  • Once this is recieved, your registration is complete.
  • In the case your registration is not approved and a response is given, correct the mistake as advised and re-apply
 The waiting period this for Students who have filled the registration form but whose registrations have not yet been approved.
4 You are Now a Student!
  • Get in contact with your teachers
  • Obtain A timetable from your teachers
 You must have completed a registration and it has been approved during your waiting period!
