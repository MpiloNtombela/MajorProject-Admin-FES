# Account Type Description Who can request it?
(a) Admin Account You should be a Baseline employee whose role is a site Administrator. If you request this account type yet you are clearly not expected to, you be considered as a security risk and some legal steps may be taken. Baseline Academy Site Admininstrator ONLY
(b) Manager Account You should be a Baseline employee whose role is a manager or related to management. Please ensure that you qualify for this account by confirming with your employer or the site administrator. Baseline Academy Management staff ONLY
(c) Normal User Account This is the defalut account you have when you register. Students are kindly advised to feel comfortable with this account and not request any other account type, other than a Student Account You already have this if you signed up
(d) Student Account After Signing up, one may opt to register as a student. You are highly encouraged to do so. Anyone who has a normal user account
